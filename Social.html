Who were able to answer questions give and good advice on how to tackle life at university and just life being a computing student in year 1. Personally, they comforted me in areas I was scared about such as tests and assessments.

#Me peer support -This is another support group. We are a group of 6-8 people and we mainly just talk about how our day/week went. We do this to relieve stress and just to have a forum to talk to people. That is very helpful when you are feeling down. We write in our logbook per session and reflect on it in the next session. At the end of the session, we do stretches together and afterwards we talk about how we felt doing it. This a nice and easy time to just cool off.
